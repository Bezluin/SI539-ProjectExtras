Burton Memorial Tower

Landmark Type

University Clock Tower

Year Built

1936

Introduction

One of the most iconic strucutres at the University of Michigan campus, the Burton Memorial Tower is located at Ingalls Mall on the central campus.

The tower houses a grand carillon that is siad to be the fourth-heaviest in the world, weighing 43 tons in total.

Students on central campus can hear the ringing of the bell tower often between classes. The tower also has lighting within it, giving it a completely different look at night, with more than 100 LED bulbs that can be programmed in various colors.
