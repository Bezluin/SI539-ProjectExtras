The Michigan League

Landmark Type

Student Union

Year Built

1929

Introduction

The Michigan League is one of the three major student unions on campus. Numerous student activities and events take place in this building, including career fairs and orientation activities/ceremonies.

Within the building, there are meeting rooms, study spaces, a theatre, a ballroom, and fast food restaurants/cafes.
