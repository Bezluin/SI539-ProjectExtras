About The Website

UMich Perspective is an unofficial student-made website with the purpose of sharing the beauty and history of the University of Michigan campus. It presents a collection of photos viewing the campus from the eyes of a current student and provides historical context and introductions to some landmarks within the university. The website is in no way officially affiliated with the University


About The Author

My name is Benjamin Tse-Bung Yu, and I am a current masters student at the University of Michigan School of Information. I specialize in Human-Computer Interaction and my primary areas of interest include UX research/design and information retrieval.

As a fanatic of university lore and architecture, I wish to share the beauty and history of the University with others through this website.
